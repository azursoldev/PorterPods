Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PorterPods and our modular building process.

PorterPods are high-quality, 12' × 24' modular sections designed to be combined into custom home layouts. They are built in a controlled factory environment and shipped to your site for final assembly.

Our system is highly scalable. You can start with a single pod (288 sq. ft.) and grow up to 8+ bedrooms and 4 bathrooms by adding more pods. There's virtually no limit to how many pods can be connected.

While site preparation varies, the actual manufacturing of your pods typically takes 4-8 weeks. Once delivered, on-site assembly and finishing can often be completed in just a few days.
